Industry article · Trade & policy

The impact of US tariffs on the luxury watch market: a blessing in disguise for some brands?

IFM Business School · February 2026
The sweeping US tariffs on Swiss goods in 2025 — peaking at 39% before settling at 15% — did not destroy the American watch market. Instead, they acted as a catalyst for structural realignment, strengthening the most prestigious brands while squeezing mid-tier players and their retail partners. Documents brand-by-brand price increases and examines why Patek Philippe's partial reversal makes it the exception that proves the rule.
Keywords: US tariffs · luxury pricing · Swiss watch exports · Rolex · Patek Philippe · margins
